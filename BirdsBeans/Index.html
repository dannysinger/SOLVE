Birds and Beans

Sustain Yourself

We warmly invite you to enjoy some of life's simple pleasures.

Admire the art created by your neighbours from Etobicoke-Lakeshore.Notice the benches and kneeling wall repurposed from the historical Christ Church Mimico after their tragic fire.Converse with your neighbours, read a book or surf the net on our free WiFi. Enjoy the setting by the newly transformed Amos Waites Park. Wander out the back and enjoy your coffee by the lake with the dog walkers. Now put these all together with a boutique brewed coffee or perfectly poured espresso or lattéand top it off with a home baked chocolate chunk cookie.
